FAQs

What types of events does Dinho cater?

Dinho Catering specializes in catering for a variety of events, including weddings, corporate events, private parties, and more.

Can Dinho accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate a variety of dietary restrictions, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. Please let us know in advance of any dietary restrictions or allergies, so we can ensure that your guests are well taken care of.

How far in advance should I book Dinho for my event?

It is best to book Dinho as far in advance as possible to ensure availability for your desired date. However, we will do our best to accommodate last-minute requests if possible.

What is included in Dinho's services?

Dinho's services include menu planning, food preparation, and on-site catering services. We also offer rental equipment and décor options, as well as service staff to ensure a seamless and enjoyable event for you and your guests.

How do I make a booking with Dinho?

To make a booking with Dinho, please contact us via phone or email to discuss your event needs and request a quote. We will work with you to customize a catering package that meets your needs and budget.
